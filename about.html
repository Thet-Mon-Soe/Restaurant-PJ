ABOUT OUR RESTAURANT

MonMaGyi Restaurant is one of the most well known and prestigious chain of casual and easy dining Myanmar Restaurant. Established in 2014 in Mandalay, it has been growing speedily and we have opened more restaurants in Yangon - Mandalay Expressway which is mostly used Expressway in Myanmar . We are one of the best Myanmar Traditional Restaurant, serving to Locals and Tourists in mutiple locations in Myanmar - Mandalay, Naypyidaw & 76 Miles Expressway with mouth-watering delights in Myanmar curries, snacks, salad and myanmar sweets, superior service and with promise of a truly amazing myanmar dining experience.

ABOUT OUR FOOD

The Award-winning Myanmar Restaurant offers truly authentic Burmese cuisine coupled with stunning myanmar ambience and service. Using ever day very fresh quality product from local market, you will be able to tuck into a range of traditional dishes. Allow yourself to try a little bit of everything. Also, we are famous in town for making wide variety of Myanmar Snacks. Non-meat eaters can delight in our carefully created vegetarian menu and everyone is sure to find something they will love on our extensive menu. Join us to enjoy the best myanmar food for Breakfast , Lunch , Dinner and Snacks every day of the week from our very experienced Myanmar Chef. “ We know how to make a great tasting Myanmar Food”
