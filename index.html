ABOUT US

Mon Ma Gyi Restaurant is in Yangon. It will make you feel right at home. We are rated No.1 for all restaurants in Yangon on Tripadvisor in 2020,2021 and 2022.This restaurant is opened in April 2019 and it is a mix of modern and traditional interior with comfy, functional furnishings give this open-plan space an agreeable symmetry: row upon row of dining tables are neatly arranged across a teak table and chair . The 250-seat restaurant draws its inspiration, serving a broad selection of Myanmar foods at respectable prices.
